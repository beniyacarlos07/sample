ANG MGA KARAKTER SA NOLI ME TANGERE (Beniya Carlos 9 - Topaz)
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Crisostomo Ibarra is the main character of the novel and he is the son of Don Rafael, and he returned from Europe after studying there for 7 years. He learned that his father has passed away and Ibarra is a well-respected gentleman.
Maria Clara She is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and Padre Damaso in Noli Me Tangere. She is a beautiful woman and the childhood sweetheart of Crisostomo Ibarra in the story.
Elias Elias is a friend of Crisostomo Ibarra's and who resents from the Catholic Church and the Spanish government. The main protagonist of the story, Ibarra, saves Elias from a crocodile and Elias swore to protect the man from his enemies.
Padre Damaso He is the main antagonist in Noli Me Tangere and the real father of Maria Clara. He is known as the Spanish friar, who is a cruel, arrogant priest in the story. He despises Crisostomo Ibarra, for he does not want him to marry his daughter without his permission. Padre Damaso insulted Don Rafael towards Ibarra, which almost caused the death of the priest as Ibarra was offended.
Kapitan Tiago He was born in Malabon, althought his father declined to pay for his school fees. In result, Tiago was sent to school by a Dominican. After Tiago's father passed away, he started working and married Dona Pia Alba. After 6 years, Dona Pia Alba visited the shrine in Obando and she became pregnant and died giving birth to her daughter, Maria Clara. Tiya Isabel, the sister of Tiago, helped raise Maria Clara with Kapitan Tiago.
